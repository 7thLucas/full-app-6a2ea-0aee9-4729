Why GlobePin Exists

The Problem & The Answer

⚡

Location Sharing Is Broken

  • — Map links fail across platforms — wrong format, wrong app, device mismatches
  • — Screenshots lose quality and context before reaching the recipient
  • — Flat map images feel static — no sense of place, scale, or spatial beauty
  • — Recipients often need the same app installed just to open a shared link
🌍

Pin Once, Share Everywhere

  • ✓ Interactive 3D globe for spatial, immersive location discovery
  • ✓ Universal share links that open and render correctly on every platform
  • ✓ No app required on the recipient's side — tap the link, explore the place
  • ✓ One-tap to WhatsApp, Instagram, X, SMS, email, iMessage, and beyond
