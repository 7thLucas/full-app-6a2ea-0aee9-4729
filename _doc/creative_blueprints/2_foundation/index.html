Why GlobePin exists

⚠️ The Problem

Location sharing is broken

  • ✕ Map links routinely break — wrong format, wrong app, device incompatibilities
  • ✕ Screenshots lose quality and provide zero spatial context
  • ✕ Recipient must have the same app installed — or the link simply fails
  • ✕ Flat static maps can't convey the scale and magic of a great place
🌍 The Solution

One exact pin. One link. Works everywhere.

  • ✓ Drop a pin on exact GPS coordinates on a photorealistic 3D globe
  • ✓ Every pin generates a universal link — no app required on the recipient's side
  • ✓ One-tap share to WhatsApp, Instagram, X, SMS, email, iMessage and more
  • ✓ Tourists discover; locals curate — both share, both receive, zero friction
